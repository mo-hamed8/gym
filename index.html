Equipment Guide

chest press

This exercise involves using a lever machine to perform a chest press movement. The user sits on the machine and pushes the handles forward, engaging the chest muscles. The lever machine provides resistance throughout the movement.

Muscles Targeted

Quadriceps Glutes Hamstrings Calves

How to Use the Machine

1

Sit on the machine with your back and head resting against the padded support

2

Place your feet on the platform about hip-width apart

3

Release the safety bars and slowly lower the platform by bending your knees

Common Mistakes to Avoid

!

Locking knees at full extension - this puts unnecessary stress on the joints

!

Placing feet too high or too low on the platform - affects muscle engagement
